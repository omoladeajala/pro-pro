    RICE

    Ingredient

  • Raw rice
  • Water
  • Pot
  • Salt
  • Green leaf
  • Oil

    Steps in cooking rice:

  1. Turn on your gas
  2. Put your water on fire for about 10 minutes
  3. Rinse your rice thrice
  4. Pour the rinde rice inside the boiling water
  5. Add oil and small amount of salt

    Beans

    Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Maggi
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Palm oil

    Steps in cooking beans:

  1. Put water inside pot and put it on fire.
  2. Rinse your picked beans.
  3. Pour it inside the water on fire.
  4. After about 50 minutes, add your pepper.
  5. After few minutes, add your palm oil.
  6. After 15 minutes, serve your beans to eat.
